Organic’s Nutrition Importance

Organic foods are grown without the use of pesticides and synthetic fertilizers. Organic meats, poultry, dairy and eggs all come from animals that have not been raised on antibiotics or growth hormones. Research has shown that organically grown fruits and vegetables retain greater nutritional value than foods grown with pesticides and other chemicals.
